Product Features

Engineered for the caesarean theatre

  • Surgeon gowns made of medical-grade SMS fabric, meeting AAMI Level 3 barrier protection — preventing blood strike-through & fluid contamination.
  • Large & wide surgical drape to sufficiently cover the patient.
  • Ultrasonically sealed seams prevent strike-through.
  • Caesarean drape comes with a wide collection pouch and suction port.
  • Sufficient fenestration with incise — easier accessibility while retaining sterility of un-accessed fenestrations.
  • EO sterilised per ISO 11135, using medical-grade packaging that meets ISO 11607-1 and EN 868-5 requirements.
